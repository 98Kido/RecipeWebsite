Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pancetta
  • Black pepper

Instructions

1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and cheese.

3. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Add drained spaghetti and mix.

4. Remove from heat and add the egg mixture, stirring quickly.

5. Serve immediately, topped with black pepper.
